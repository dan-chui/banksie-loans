About Us

Welcome to Banksie Loans. Thank you for your interest in learning more about us.

Banksie Loans is a regional bank holding company with $50 billion in assets headquartered in San Francisco, CA. Founded in 1972, Banksie Loans and its affiliates provide consumers, small and middle-market businesses, corporations, municipalities, and other organizations with a comprehensive suite of banking, payments, wealth management, and risk management products and services. Banksie Loans operates more than 50 branches in the State of California, with certain businesses operating in extended geographies.
